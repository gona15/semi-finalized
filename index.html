Frequently Asked Questions

Real concerns from business owners

How do time zones work if you're in Iraq?

I work Iraq time (GMT+3) but I'm flexible. Most communication happens via WhatsApp/email anyway. For calls, I adjust to your schedule - I've worked with clients from Australia to California. The 24-hour audit delivery promise isn't affected by time zones.

Is it safe to work with someone overseas?

Absolutely. I use Upwork's escrow system for extra protection - your money is held by Upwork until you approve the work. I've completed 50+ international projects with perfect ratings. You can also pay via Stripe for familiar payment processing.

What if Facebook shuts down my ad account?

I follow Facebook's policies strictly and have never had a client's account banned. If issues arise, I know how to resolve them quickly. I also set up backup assets and use compliant tracking methods to minimize any risks.

Do you require contracts or long-term commitments?

Never. Month-to-month billing only. You should keep me because I'm delivering results, not because you're trapped in a contract. If you're not happy with my work, fire me. That's how it should be.

What's the difference between ad spend and your fee?

My fee ($797/month) is for strategy, setup, optimization, and reporting. Your ad spend ($800-2000/month) goes directly to Facebook for showing your ads. Total investment: $1,597-2,797/month. You control the ad budget and can see exactly where every dollar goes.

Who owns the ads, landing pages, and data?

You own everything. The Facebook ad account is yours, landing pages are built on your domain, and all data belongs to you. If you ever stop working with me, you keep all the assets I've created.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see increased leads within 2-3 weeks. Significant revenue impact typically happens at 60-90 days as I optimize the entire funnel. The 30-day guarantee ensures you see movement within a month or get refunded.

What if my business type is unique?

The fundamentals work for any service business: attract attention, build trust, make compelling offers, follow up consistently. I've worked with dentists, chiropractors, gyms, salons, contractors, and consultants. The tactics adapt, but the strategy remains the same.
