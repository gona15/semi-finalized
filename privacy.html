Privacy Policy

At ArmanLeads, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use my marketing services or website.

Last Updated: January 2025

Information I Collect

Information You Provide

When you contact me or use my services, I may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, business name, phone number
  • Business Information: Business type, website URL, marketing challenges, service requirements
  • Communication Records: Messages sent via contact forms, email, WhatsApp, or calls
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through Stripe or Upwork (I don't store payment card details)

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit my website, I may collect:

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources, click patterns
  • Cookies: Small text files that help improve your experience (see Cookies section below)

How I Use Your Information

I use your personal information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: Providing marketing audits, consultations, and ongoing services
  • Communication: Responding to inquiries, sending project updates, and providing support
  • Business Operations: Processing payments, managing client relationships, and maintaining records
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing website usage to improve user experience and service quality
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements

I do not: Sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

I may share your information in limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Formspree: Form processing service that handles contact form submissions
  • Stripe: Payment processing for invoices and transactions
  • Upwork: Escrow services for secure project payments
  • Email Providers: For business communication and project correspondence

Legal Requirements

I may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect my rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Investigate potential violations of terms of service

Business Transfers

If I sell or transfer my business, your information may be transferred to the new owner, subject to the same privacy protections.

Data Retention

I retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide ongoing services and support
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations (typically 7 years for business records)
  • Resolve disputes or enforce agreements
  • Maintain legitimate business records

Contact form submissions and general inquiries are typically retained for 2 years unless you request deletion or become a client.

Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data transmission is secured using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Storage: Data stored with reputable cloud providers with strong security practices
  • Regular Updates: Systems and software are kept up-to-date with security patches

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. I cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any significant data breaches as required by law.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

General Rights (Available to All Users)

  • Access: Request information about what personal data I have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of non-essential communications

Additional Rights (EU/EEA Residents - GDPR)

  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Processing Restriction: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Additional Rights (California Residents - CCPA)

  • Know: Right to know what personal information is collected and how it's used
  • Delete: Right to delete personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: Right not to be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

To Exercise Your Rights: Contact me at hello@armanleads.com with your request. I'll respond within 30 days and may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

Cookies and Tracking

My website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and user experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Session management and security
  • Contact form functionality
  • Basic site preferences

Analytics Cookies

  • Website usage statistics (anonymized where possible)
  • Performance monitoring
  • User behavior analysis for site improvements

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.

International Data Transfers

As I'm based in Iraq and serve clients globally, your information may be transferred and processed in different countries. When transferring data internationally, I ensure adequate protection through:

  • Using service providers with appropriate privacy certifications
  • Implementing contractual safeguards
  • Following applicable data protection laws
  • Obtaining your consent where required

Children's Privacy

My services are not directed at children under 16. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If I become aware that I've collected information from a child under 16, I will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in my practices or legal requirements. When I make significant changes:

  • I'll notify active clients by email if changes materially affect their rights
  • I'll post the updated policy on my website

Your continued use of my services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Legal Basis for Processing (EU/EEA)

For EU/EEA residents, I process personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interests: Business operations, fraud prevention, and service improvements
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Consent: Where you've provided specific consent (which you can withdraw)
