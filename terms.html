1. Services Overview

ArmanLeads provides performance marketing services specifically for dentists, gyms, medical practices, and local service businesses. My services include:

Core Services

  • Marketing Audits: Comprehensive analysis of current marketing efforts and opportunities
  • Facebook & Instagram Advertising: Campaign setup, management, and optimization
  • Landing Page Creation: High-converting pages designed for your specific business type
  • Conversion Tracking: Facebook Pixel, Conversions API, and Google Analytics setup
  • Follow-up Systems: Email sequences and automated appointment reminders
  • Performance Reporting: Weekly reports with actionable insights and next steps
  • Strategic Consultation: Ongoing advice and optimization recommendations

What's Not Included

  • Ad spend budget (paid directly to platforms by you)
  • Content creation for social media posts
  • SEO or Google Ads management (unless specifically agreed)
  • Graphic design beyond landing page layouts
  • Website development outside of landing pages

3. Performance Guarantee & Refund Policy

30-Day "No Movement" Guarantee

If you don't see measurable improvement in your marketing performance within 30 days of campaign launch, you're eligible for a full refund of the setup fee, provided:

What "Movement" Means:

  • Increase in qualified leads by at least 15% compared to baseline
  • Improvement in cost per lead by at least 20%
  • Increase in landing page conversion rate by at least 25%
  • Higher appointment booking rate from generated leads

Client Cooperation Required:

  • Timely Access: Provide necessary account access within 48 hours of request
  • Prompt Approvals: Review and approve campaigns, landing pages within 72 hours
  • Adequate Budget: Maintain minimum $800/month ad spend as discussed
  • Implementation: Implement recommended changes to booking/follow-up processes
  • Communication: Respond to requests for information within 2 business days
  • Platform Compliance: Maintain compliant business practices and ad account standing

Refund Process

To request a refund under the guarantee:

  1. Submit written request within 35 days of campaign launch
  2. Provide access to analytics data for performance verification
  3. Complete exit interview to identify improvement opportunities
  4. Refund processed within 14 business days via original payment method

Guarantee Exclusions

The guarantee does not apply if:

  • Client fails to meet cooperation requirements above
  • External factors prevent campaign execution (platform bans, policy changes)
  • Client modifies campaigns or landing pages without approval
  • Budget is reduced below agreed minimums during guarantee period
  • Client operates in prohibited industries or uses non-compliant practices
